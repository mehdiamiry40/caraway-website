How to Sell a Damaged Car in Brisbane: Complete Guide

Accident damaged, mechanical issues, or flood affected? Here's how to still get good cash for your damaged vehicle.

Many Brisbane car owners don't realize their damaged vehicle still has significant value. Whether your car has been in an accident, has mechanical problems, or is simply too old to repair economically, you can still get cash for it.

Types of Damaged Cars We Buy

Accident Damaged Vehicles

  • Minor fender benders and dents
  • Major collision damage
  • Written-off vehicles (repairable and statutory write-offs)
  • Hail damaged cars

Mechanical Issues

  • Engine problems (blown head gasket, timing chain, etc.)
  • Transmission failure
  • Electrical faults
  • Overheating issues
  • Cars that won't start

Other Damage Types

  • Flood or water damage
  • Fire damage
  • Storm damage
  • Vandalism
  • Rust and corrosion

üí° Good News

Even cars that seem worthless have value. Parts can be salvaged, metals recycled, and some vehicles can be repaired. Cash for cars services see value where others don't.

How Much Can You Get for a Damaged Car?

Damaged car values in Brisbane typically range from:

  • Minor damage (driveable): $1,000 - $5,000
  • Moderate damage: $500 - $2,500
  • Major damage (non-driveable): $200 - $1,500
  • Complete write-off: $100 - $800

Factors that affect damaged car value:

  • Make and model (popular brands = more valuable parts)
  • Age of the vehicle
  • Extent of damage
  • Current scrap metal prices
  • Demand for parts

The Process: Selling Your Damaged Car

  1. Get a quote: Call or submit details online. Be honest about the damage.

Tips for Selling a Damaged Car

  • Be honest: Accurately describe all damage
  • Take photos: Document the damage clearly
  • Keep the keys: Even if the car doesn't run
  • Have your ID ready: Required for the sale
  • Remove personal items: Check thoroughly before pickup
