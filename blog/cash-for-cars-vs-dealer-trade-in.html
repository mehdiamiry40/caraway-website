Cash for Cars: The Details

Advantages

  • Better prices: Typically 10-30% more than trade-in offers
  • No purchase required: Sell without buying another car
  • Any condition: Accept damaged, old, or non-running vehicles
  • Convenience: They come to your location
  • Cash in hand: Immediate payment
  • Free removal: No towing costs

Disadvantages

  • Need to find a reputable service
  • May need to get multiple quotes

Dealer Trade-In: The Details

Advantages

  • One-stop shop: Sell and buy in one place
  • Tax benefits: May reduce stamp duty on new car
  • Simple process: Dealer handles everything

Disadvantages

  • Lower offers: Dealers need profit margin
  • Must buy from them: Only works if purchasing
  • Pressure tactics: May use trade-in to confuse pricing
  • Limited for old/damaged cars: May refuse or offer very low

üí° Pro Tip

Even if you plan to trade in, get a cash for cars quote first. Use it as leverage to negotiate a better trade-in price, or simply take the higher offer!

When to Choose Cash for Cars

  • You want the most money for your car
  • You're not buying a new car immediately
  • Your car is older, damaged, or has high mileage
  • You want a quick, hassle-free sale
  • You prefer cash payment

When to Choose Trade-In

  • You're definitely buying from that dealer
  • The stamp duty savings outweigh the price difference
  • Convenience is your top priority
  • The dealer offers a competitive price
