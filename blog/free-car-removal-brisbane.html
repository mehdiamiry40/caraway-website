Free Car Removal Brisbane: What to Expect & How It Works

Published: January 20, 2025 5 min read

Need to get rid of an unwanted car but worried about towing costs? Good news: legitimate cash for cars services in Brisbane offer genuinely free car removal—and they'll pay YOU. Here's exactly what to expect from the process.

Is Free Car Removal Really Free?

Yes, 100%. When you sell to a reputable cash for cars service, there are no hidden fees:

  • No towing charges
  • No pickup fees
  • No paperwork processing costs
  • No "administration" charges

⚠️ Red Flag Warning

If any company tries to deduct "towing fees" from your quote after arriving, that's a bait-and-switch scam. Walk away. Reputable services include ALL costs in your initial quote.

The Free Car Removal Process: Step by Step

Step 1: Get Your Quote (5 minutes)

Contact us by phone or online form with basic details:

  • Make, model, and year
  • Approximate condition
  • Whether it's running or not
  • Your Brisbane suburb

You'll receive an instant quote—no obligations.

Step 2: Accept & Schedule (2 minutes)

Happy with the offer? We'll arrange a pickup time that suits you:

  • Same-day pickup: Often available if you call before 2pm
  • Scheduled pickup: Choose a specific day and time window
  • Weekend service: Available for your convenience

Step 3: We Come to You

Our team arrives at your location with:

  • Appropriate tow truck (flatbed for non-runners)
  • All necessary equipment
  • Cash payment ready
  • Transfer paperwork prepared

Step 4: Quick Inspection (5 minutes)

We do a brief visual check to confirm the car matches what was described. As long as there are no major undisclosed issues (like missing engine parts), your quote stands.

Step 5: Paperwork & Payment (10 minutes)

We handle all the paperwork:

  • Transfer of ownership documentation
  • Receipt of sale
  • Any necessary Queensland Transport forms

You receive your cash payment on the spot—count it, it's yours.

Step 6: Car Removed

We load the car and drive away. The whole process from arrival to departure typically takes 15-30 minutes.

What You Need to Have Ready

To ensure a smooth pickup, please have:

  • Photo ID: Driver's licence or passport
  • Proof of ownership: Registration papers, receipt of purchase, or equivalent
  • Keys: If available (not essential for scrap cars)
  • Personal belongings removed: Check glove box, boot, under seats

Common Questions

What if my car doesn't start?

No problem! We bring flatbed tow trucks that can load non-running vehicles. Just let us know when booking so we send the right equipment.

What if my car is on private property?

We can access most properties. If there are unusual access challenges (narrow lanes, steep driveways), mention this when booking and we'll advise.

Can you remove a car from a garage/carport?

Yes, as long as there's enough clearance for our equipment. We can push/roll non-running cars out of tight spaces.

What if I'm not the registered owner?

You'll need written authorisation from the registered owner, or documentation proving you're entitled to sell (deceased estate, repossession, etc.).

Why We Offer Free Removal

You might wonder how we can afford free towing. Simple: your car has value to us whether running or not. We recover that value through:

  • Reselling running vehicles
  • Parting out valuable components
  • Recycling metals and materials

The removal cost is built into our business model—you never pay a cent.
