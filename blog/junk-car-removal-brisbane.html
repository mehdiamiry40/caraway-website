What Affects Junk Car Value?

1. Metal Weight

Larger, heavier vehicles contain more recyclable steel and are worth more. A full-size 4WD will fetch more than a compact hatchback.

2. Salvageable Parts

Even in junk cars, certain parts hold value:

  • Catalytic converters (contain precious metals)
  • Alloy wheels
  • Working transmissions and engines
  • Body panels in good condition
  • Electronics and entertainment systems

3. Make & Model Demand

Popular brands like Toyota, Mazda, and Holden have higher parts demand, meaning better prices even for junk vehicles.

4. Current Scrap Metal Prices

Steel prices fluctuate with global markets. Brisbane prices in 2025 are relatively strong due to continued infrastructure projects.
